$90 gets you 90 Days Of Accountability

We all want to move up and make more money, but we all struggle with the needed daily leetcode grind required to go into leet code interviews knowing that we'll ace them. *Leetgrind For Humanity* is a non-profit 90-day practice period that costs $90 to participate in.

Commit To A Schedule

You commit to a schedule each week, for 90 days, log into a Zoom meeting with fellow participants and study on your own or in a Zoom breakout and get monitored for attendance.

Fame Or Shame: Your Choice

A "Wall of Fame" tracks who is logging the most hours each week; a "Wall of Shame" tracks who was kicked out of the practice period for tardiness or skipped attendance.

Grinding For Humanity

Either way at the end of the 90 days your money is donated to a humane cause like The Ukraine Defense Fund or similar. And of course you come out a Leet Beast ready to get paid.
