$90 gets you 90 Days Of Accountability

We all want to move up and make more money, but we all struggle with the needed daily leetcode grind required to go into leetcode interviews knowing that we'll ace them. Leetgrind For Humanity is a non-profit 90-day practice period that costs $90 to participate in.

Commit To A Schedule

You commit to a schedule each week, for 90 days, log into a Zoom meeting at the times on your schedul with fellow participants, and study, either quietly in the main Zoom room or in a breakout, and ultimately get monitored for attendance. If you are late or miss more than twice you are kicked out of the current practice period.

Fame Or Shame: Your Choice

A "Wall of Fame" tracks who is logging the most hours each week; a "Wall of Shame" tracks who was kicked out of the practice period for tardiness or skipped attendance.

Grinding For Humanity

Either way, at the end of the 90 days your money is donated to a humane cause like The Ukraine Defense Fund or similar. And of course, you come out a LeetBeast ready to get paid.
